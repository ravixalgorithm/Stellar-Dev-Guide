About Stellar

Stellar is an open-source, decentralized protocol for digital currency to fiat currency transfers which allows cross-border transactions between any currencies. Stellar makes it easy to create, send, and trade digital representations of all forms of money: dollars, pesos, bitcoin, pretty much anything.

Why Use Stellar?

  • Fast Transactions: Stellar can handle thousands of transactions per second with a confirmation time of 3-5 seconds.
  • Low Fees: Stellar’s transaction fees are very low, making it cost-effective for users.
  • Decentralized: Stellar operates on a decentralized network, ensuring security and reliability.
  • Interoperability: Stellar supports a wide range of assets and currencies, facilitating seamless cross-border payments.
